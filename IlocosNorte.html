“Boracay of the North”

Destination Unique Feature Brief Description
ILOCOS NORTE Pagudpud is a coastal town located at the northernmost tip of Ilocos Norte. It is known as the “Boracay of the North”, but it has so much more to offer apart from its pristine coastline. Pagudpud's Bangui Bay also features 20 wind turbines, which makes it Southeast Asia's first windmill farm. Pagudpud is famous for its breathtaking white sandy beaches, with Saud Beach being the most well-known. The beach features powdery white sand that stretches for kilometers along the coastline. Crystal-clear waters and gentle waves make it an ideal destination for swimming, sunbathing, and beachcombing.
