Destination Unique Feature Brief Description
BORACAY Beautiful landscapes, crystal clear waters, sugary beaches, and lush hilly landscapes into an idyllic tourist haven. Boracay is a tropical island in the Philippines that is famous for its white sand beaches, clear blue waters, and relaxing atmosphere. It is located in the province of Aklan, about an hour's flight from Manila or Cebu. Boracay is one of the world's top destinations for relaxation and has won multiple awards. The island also offers various adventures and activities for visitors of all ages.
